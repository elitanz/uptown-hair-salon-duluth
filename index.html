Welcome to Uptown

A Piedmont Avenue institution

Since 1995, Uptown Hair Salon has been the friendly, full-service salon on Duluth's Piedmont Avenue — a clean, welcoming room where a team of stylists of every age takes real pride in sending you home looking exactly how you pictured.

From a precision cut to a full color transformation, extensions, a fresh manicure or a wax, it's the kind of place regulars keep coming back to for the people as much as the work.
